## Design Context

### Users
- **Customers**: High-end gastronomy enthusiasts looking for a private chef experience. They expect a premium, sensory-rich, and seamless browsing/ordering experience on both mobile and desktop.
- **Chef Rolando**: The primary administrator who manages the menu, site content, and reviews. Needs a clean, efficient, and intuitive dashboard.

### Brand Personality
- **Luxury**: Premium, exclusive, and high-end feel.
- **Sensory**: Focus on visual appeal and immersive transitions.
- **Authentic**: Reflecting the craftsmanship of 主廚 Rolando.
- **Professional**: Reliable management tools for the backend.

### Aesthetic Direction
- **Theme**: Luxury Dark (Deep black base with Gold accents).
- **Typography**: `Noto Serif TC` for elegant display headings and `Noto Sans TC` for clean, readable body text.
- **Imagery**: High-quality, professional food photography is central to the design.
- **Vibe**: Sophisticated, refined, and immersive. Avoid generic templates; focus on custom micro-animations and smooth transitions.

### Design Principles
- **Elegance over Clutter**: Maintain a high signal-to-noise ratio. Only show what's necessary, but make it beautiful.
- **Mobile-First Luxury**: Ensure the premium feel is preserved on small screens with thumb-friendly controls and responsive layouts.
- **Interactive Depth**: Use subtle animations (shimmer, fade-in-up, scale-down) to guide the user and add a sense of life.
- **Consistency**: Maintain the black-and-gold palette and serif/sans pairing across both the public site and the management back-end.
- **Security & Trust**: Clear feedback for administrative actions and secure data management.
